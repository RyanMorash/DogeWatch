    Welcome to DogeWatch.

    DogeWatch is an app for the Pebble smartwatch that lets you check the current price of 1 DOGE. Using this app you simply need to select "DogeWatch" from your Pebble's menu and you will be instantly shown DogeCoin's progress to the moon.
